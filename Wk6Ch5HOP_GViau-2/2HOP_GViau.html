Types of Architecture

  1. Cape Cod Style
    • Classic American home style
    • Originated in New England
  2. Federal Style
    • Refined American version of Neoclassical architecture
    • Usually with symmetry, simple shapes, and fine details with Roman inspirations
  3. Victorian Style
    • Consists of highly ornamented styles
    • Flourished in the United Kingdom
  4. Shingle Style
    • Late 19th century American style
    • Creates a sculptural and naturalist ascetic
